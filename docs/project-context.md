# Project Context

Date started: 2026-05-30

## Founder intent

Simon and Uli want to explore a modern, innovative, highly attractive online-first distribution / ecommerce company for preparedness products and related utensils, initially focused on Germany.

The ambition is to differentiate strongly from existing prepper/survival shops through better design, clearer communication, cutting-edge marketing, and more thoughtful product architecture.

## First strategic question

What is the strongest possible starting point after a thorough market scan across Germany, Europe, the US, and global benchmarks?

## Early positioning direction

Not hardcore prepper, not army surplus, not fear marketing. More likely: calm, serious, accessible preparedness for normal households, families, urban professionals, and organizations.
